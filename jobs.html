Ideal Jobs

Below we summarise the ideal jobs for our team from Assignment 1 and provide commentary on similarities and differences between the ideal jobs and our current career paths.

Michael Reyland

General Manager – Renewable Energy

The job is a general manager position, the technical and general skills needed for the position is broad in scope with a degree in engineering, commerce, economics or finance and experience in construction or project management. Aspects of the ideal job are; leadership is required for the role being a general management position overseeing; finances; human resources; project development, commercial and technical functions; sales to generate growth and maintain relationships with new or existing clientele.

Grant Dawson

Service Owner - Telecommunications

The job position is in telecommunications as a contract holder overseeing field-based deployments and support. The job requires a degree in information technology with ITIL, networking certifications and project management expertise. The position entails management of; vendors with corresponding service level agreements; technical leadership; root cause analysis and escalation; performance programs; communication with involved stakeholders and overseeing changes and integration of ICT projects.

Ben Tomlinson

C# Programmer and Unity Developer

This job requires completion of a Bachelor of Computer Science or equivalent, extensive knowledge of C# and version control, experience in unity, AWS and VR technologies. The job entails development, testing and deployment of VR, troubleshooting issues and ensuring security of the platform.

Job Comparison

There are common elements in each of the positions as the general manager position and Service Owner Telecommunication energy resources sector entails management, but the technical side differs. The security analyst and ethical hacking positions are very similar as the purpose of both positions is to find weaknesses to strengthen infrastructure but use differing methods. An analyst’s purpose is to prevent breaches where as an ethical hacker or penetration tester seeks to gain entry and breach a system. The other similarities are skills and experience in networking, security and general IT skills. C# and unity developer and ethical hacking use programming but in different contexts. Overall there are similarities in requirements and soft skills, but only the security analyst and penetration tester are following a similar career path, as for the rest they are different.
